About this site

This site was designed

by Rachel Shorey. She's a software developer at Sunlight Foundation, but she worked on this site in her spare time. Rachel was a Girl Scout for 6 years as a scout (Troop 1924) at 2 years as troop leader (Troop 60821).

The data used here

came from The Sunlight Foundation, a nonprofit organization devoted to government accountability and transparency.

Interested in helping

make this site better or more informative, or in learning more about how it was made? See the code on github where you can submit an issue if you have an idea or notice a problem. Or contact Rachel by email or on twitter with ideas or to find out more!

No one works alone

and I am especially lucky to have many wonderful collaborators, mentors and friends who helped me out with this project or gave me ideas, especially Shannon Turner and Olivia Cheng.
